Ming-Feng Li (Justin)

Carnegie Mellon University
Robotics Institute, School of Computer Science

Hi, I'm Justin, a first-year graduate student in Robotic Systems Development (MRSD) at Carnegie Mellon University. Before joining CMU, I earned my M.S. degree in Electrical Engineering from National Tsing Hua University under the guidance of Prof. Min Sun. My research centers on 3D generation and reconstruction methods using NeRF (neural radiance fields for view synthesis) and pre-trained VLMs (vision-language models). My interests span the intersection of computer vision and robotics, including computer vision, 3D generation, 3D reconstruction, and VLMs. I am actively exploring internship opportunities for 2024 Summer, please feel free to contact me.
